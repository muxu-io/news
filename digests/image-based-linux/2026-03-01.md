### Kairos

*   **Kairos v4.0.0 Released:** This major release focuses on standardizing artifacts around Hadron, deprecating distribution-specific artifacts in favor of user-defined build pipelines for increased control. ARM artifact coverage is expanded to include generic, rpi4, and NVIDIA Jetson targets. ([v4.0.0](https://github.com/kairos-io/kairos/releases/tag/v4.0.0))
*   **Kairos v4.0.0-RC2, RC1:** Pre-release versions focusing on tooling and scanning improvements. ([v4.0.0-RC2](https://github.com/kairos-io/kairos/releases/tag/v4.0.0-RC2), [v4.0.0-RC1](https://github.com/kairos-io/kairos/releases/tag/v4.0.0-RC1))
*   **Kairos v4.0.0-beta:** Grype scanning is enabled on NVIDIA Jetson models. ([v4.0.0-beta2](https://github.com/kairos-io/kairos/releases/tag/v4.0.0-beta2), [v4.0.0-beta1](https://github.com/kairos-io/kairos/releases/tag/v4.0.0-beta1))

### Fedora CoreOS

*   **Zincati Upgrade Stall:** A missing `fedora-coreos.stream` annotation caused an upgrade stall in the `testing` stream. A workaround involving stopping Zincati, cleaning rpm-ostree, and restarting Zincati is documented. ([CoreOS] testing Stream: Zincati Upgrade Stall and Workaround)
*   **Podman 5.8.0 in Next Stream:** Podman 5.8.0, which includes an automatic migration from BoltDB to SQLite, is being rolled out to the `next` stream. This is a crucial transition as BoltDB support will be removed in Podman 6.0. Users are encouraged to test the migration and report issues. ([CoreOS] Podman 5.8.0 shipping in next stream for database migration)
*   **Expanding Ignition with Cloud-Specific Configuration for Azure:** The Fedora CoreOS community discussed expanding Ignition's capabilities for cloud-specific configurations on Azure. This was a dedicated video session replacing the standard community meeting. ([CoreOS] Re: Fedora CoreOS Community Video Meeting 2026-02-25)
