## Executive Summary

Several vulnerabilities require immediate attention, including a critical OS Command Injection in `@profullstack/mcp-server` and multiple unauthenticated exposures in `free5GC`. Prototype Pollution in `Velocity.js` and directory traversal in `SharpCompress` also pose significant risks. Multiple authentication bypasses in `Open WebUI` also lead to PII exposure.
