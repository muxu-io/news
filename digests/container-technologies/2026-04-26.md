The past week shows continued iteration across the container ecosystem. Moby received updates for bug fixes related to image pruning, logging, and networking, and updated containerd to v2.2.3. Podman Desktop saw two releases focusing on UI improvements and test enhancements, while crun addressed regressions in mount propagation and cgroup handling, alongside initial support for passt-based networking in microVMs with krun.
