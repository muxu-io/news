## Executive Summary

The Yocto Project 5.0.17 release is now available, including security fixes and an upgrade to OpenSSL 3.5.5. Milestone 3 for Yocto Project 6.0 has also been released. Meta-openembedded saw updates to various packages, including fixes for building with clang-22 and C23 support. Several CVEs were addressed in OpenEmbedded Core, including fixes for `libsoup`, `vim`, `glib-networking` and `libinput`.

## Critical/Urgent

- **Security:** Yocto Project 5.0.17 includes numerous security fixes, including updates to `alsa-lib`, `avahi`, `bind`, `busybox`, `freetype`, `gdk-pixbuf`, `glib-2.0`, `gnupg`, `gnutls`, `harfbuzz`, `inetutils`, `libpng` and more. Update your BSPs to include these fixes.
- **Security:** OpenSSL was upgraded to 3.5.5 in Yocto 5.0.17. Ensure your projects are compatible with this version.
