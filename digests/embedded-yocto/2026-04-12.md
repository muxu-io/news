## Executive Summary

Yocto 6.0 M3 nears completion with QA builds underway, and meta-freescale updates its U-Boot recipe to align with NXP's LF6.18.2-1.0.0 release. Several fixes for build issues caused by newer compiler standards (gnu23, autoconf 2.73/2.74) land in OE-Core and meta-openembedded. NVIDIA Jetson users report HDMI display issues and seek updated TensorFlow install guidance.

## Critical/Urgent
